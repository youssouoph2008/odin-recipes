Pancakes

Ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour 2 tablespoons white sugar 2 teaspoons baking powder ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste 1 cup milk 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 1 large egg, beaten

Directions

1. Gather the ingredients. 2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl; make a well in the center. Pour in milk, oil, and egg; mix until smooth. 3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop about ¼ cup batter per pancake onto the griddle; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 1 to 2 minutes. Flip; cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter. 4. Serve hot and enjoy!
