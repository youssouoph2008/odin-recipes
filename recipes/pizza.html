Pizza

Ingredients

1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust 1 cup spicy barbeque sauce 2 skinless boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed 1 cup sliced pepperoncini peppers 1 cup chopped red onion ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro 2 cups shredded Colby-Jack cheese

Directions

1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). 2. Place pizza crust on a baking sheet. Spread barbeque sauce on crust. 3. Top with chicken, pepperoncini peppers, onion, and cilantro. 4. Cover with Colby-Jack cheese. 5. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 15 minutes.
